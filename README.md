# Lattice Alternatives for Performance Management

Technical playbook for evaluating Lattice alternatives across performance management, employee engagement, OKRs, and people analytics.

This repository focuses on workload-based selection of people platforms, comparing specialized performance tools, engagement systems, and full HCM suites.

---

## Scope

Covers:

- Performance review systems  
- Continuous feedback and 1:1 workflows  
- OKR and goal tracking platforms  
- Employee engagement and pulse surveys  
- People analytics and talent development  
- Modular HR stack design  

Does not cover payroll-only or ATS-only tools.

---

## What Lattice Does

Lattice combines:

- Performance reviews  
- Goals and OKRs  
- 1:1 meeting workflows  
- Engagement surveys  
- Compensation planning  

It is designed to connect performance and engagement in one platform. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## Why Teams Look for Alternatives

Common triggers:

- Need for deeper engagement analytics  
- Desire for integrated learning and development  
- Preference for lightweight continuous feedback tools  
- Requirement for full HRIS or payroll integration  
- Cost and modular stack flexibility  

Many organizations move to specialized tools instead of an all-in-one suite. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Core Use Cases

### Continuous Performance Management  
Frequent feedback, check-ins, and coaching.

### OKR and Goal Alignment  
Cascading goals and measurable outcomes.

### Employee Engagement  
Pulse surveys, sentiment analysis, and action planning.

### Talent Development  
Career paths, learning plans, and succession planning.

### People Analytics  
Data-driven insights into performance and retention.

## System Categories

### Performance-First Platforms  
Focused on reviews, feedback, and goal tracking.

### Engagement-First Platforms  
Pulse surveys and employee sentiment analysis.

### Learning and Development Platforms  
Skill tracking and growth plans.

### Full HCM Suites  
Performance integrated with HRIS, payroll, and compliance.

## Evaluation Dimensions

Alternatives are compared across:

- Review workflow flexibility

- OKR alignment
